Our programmes

Sponsor a tree

When you sponsor a tree, the sponsored tree would have your name plaque on it. This could be a unique gift for someone you know.

Plant a tree

By planting a tree with this plant a tree program, our management would match and plant another tree. This 2 trees would 48 pounds of carbon dioxide per year, giving us all fresher air.

Recycling Programs

Recycling stations located throughout the Zoo encourage visitors to recycle aluminum cans and plastic bottles. Behind the scenes, staff limit landfill waste by recycling paper, wood, plastic, metal, toner cartridges and more.
